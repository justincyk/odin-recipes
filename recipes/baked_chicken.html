Baked Chicken Wings Recipe

Description:

A simple, yet delicious recipe for baked wings. Skip the unhealthy oil and go straight to baking the wings which still produce a crispy outside layer with a juicy inside.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh chicken wings
  • Butter
  • Kosher salt and black pepper
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Toss the wings in melted butter
  2. Sprinkle them with the spices
  3. Arrange the wings on a rimmed bakting sheet fitted with a wire rack
  4. Bake until fully cooked, well-browned, and crispy
