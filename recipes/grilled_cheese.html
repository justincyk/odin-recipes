Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Description

A buttery, toasty grilled cheddar cheese sandwich. When craving a great late night snack, this is the go to. Its irresistable. The cheese is stringy. You can't go wrong.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of white bread (substitute sourdough if you want to)
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices of Cheddar cheese (add more if you want)

Steps:

  1. Prehead skillet over medium heat
  2. Butter one side of a slice of bread
  3. Place buttered side down onto skillet and add 1 slice of cheese
  4. Butter a second slice of bread on one side
  5. Place butter-side up on top of sandwich
  6. Grill until lightly browned and flip over
  7. Continue grilling until cheese is melted
  8. Repeat with the remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese
