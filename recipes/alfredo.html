Alfredo Sauce Recipe

Description:

A rich and creamy alfredo sauce recipe that pairs well with red wine and garlic bread. This recipe has 4.5/5.0 stars out of over 4,000 reviews. You know from the number of reviews that this recipe will be great and you will constantly want it.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps:

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat
  2. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes
  3. Add garlic and cheese and whisk quickly
  4. Heating through. Stir in parsley and serve
